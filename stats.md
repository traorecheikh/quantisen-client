# Statistics and Analytics Displayed in the App

## AnalyticsPage.vue
- **Weekly Stock Movement Chart**: Displays entries and exits per day for the current week.
- **Total Entries**: Total number of units entered into stock for the week.
- **Total Exits**: Total number of units exited from stock for the week.
- **Net Balance**: Difference between total entries and total exits for the week.

## DashboardPage.vue
- **Total Products**: Total number of products in the inventory.
- **Low Stock Items**: Number of items with low stock.
- **Total Stock Value**: Total value of all stock (displayed in FCFA).
- **Expired Products**: Number of expired products in inventory.

These are the main statistics and analytics currently displayed in the application UI.
